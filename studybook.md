## 1. SOLUTION OVERVIEW

### Problem Statement

Traditional study material distribution lacks:
- Centralized digital storefront for selling competitive exam books
- Integrated payment processing for online sales
- Quick, secure admin controls for inventory management
- Preview mechanism for customer trust and informed purchasing

### StudyBook Solution

A **two-tier platform** delivering:

**Customer Website (Public)**
- Single-page book catalog with preview pages
- Book details (title, author, description, price)
- Shopping cart with checkout
- Shipping address collection
- UPI payment gateway integration (Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm, BHIM)
- Order confirmation & tracking

**Admin Dashboard (Secure)**
- Authenticated admin login (username/password)
- PDF upload with preview generation
- Book metadata management (edit, delete)
- Order management & analytics
- Real-time inventory control
